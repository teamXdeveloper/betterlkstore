About Better Minecraft

Better Minecraft is a popular modpack designed to enhance your vanilla Minecraft experience with carefully curated mods that improve gameplay, add quality-of-life features, and provide exciting new mechanics without breaking the classic feel.

Features

  • Optimized performance and stability
  • New biomes, mobs, and items
  • Land claiming and protection systems
  • Auction shops to trade items securely
  • Improved crafting and resource gathering
  • Regular updates with community feedback

Minimum System Requirements

  • CPU: Intel Core i3 or equivalent
  • RAM: 8 GB (recommend 12 GB for smooth experience)
  • Storage: At least 10 GB free SSD space
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 660 or AMD Radeon HD 7870 or better
  • OS: Windows 10/11, macOS 10.14+, Linux (modern distros)
  • Java: Latest Java 17 runtime (bundled with launcher)
  • Internet: Stable broadband connection for multiplayer

Recommended Settings

For the best experience on Better Minecraft:

  • Allocate 12-16 GB RAM if possible
  • Use OptiFine or Sodium for better FPS
  • Keep your GPU drivers up to date
  • Play on recommended servers like BetterLK for full features
